## Voice & Tone
- Bold and confrontational — call out excuses
- Direct response style — short, punchy, urgent
- High energy without being cringe
- Speak to action-takers, not browsers

## Do
- Use short sentences and sentence fragments
- Use urgency and scarcity when real
- Call out the reader directly ("You know this isn't working")
- Use before/after transformations as proof
- Make CTAs aggressive and specific
- Use numbers and timelines ("30lbs in 90 days")

## Don't
- Be overly polished or corporate
- Write long paragraphs
- Sound generic or templated
- Make unverifiable health claims
- Use language that could be flagged by ad platforms (Meta health policy)

## Example Tone
"You've tried the programs. You've watched the videos. You've 'started Monday' six times this year. Here's what's actually different: we don't give you information — we install a system. 500+ clients. 30lbs average in 90 days. Book the call or keep scrolling."
