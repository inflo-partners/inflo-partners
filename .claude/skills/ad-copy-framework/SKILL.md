# Ad Copy Framework — SOP

## Platform Specs

| Platform | Primary Text | Headline | Description |
|---|---|---|---|
| Meta (FB/IG) | 125 chars visible, 500 max | 40 chars | 30 chars |
| Google Search | — | 3x 30 chars | 2x 90 chars |
| YouTube | Script (first 5s critical) | — | — |

## Hook Types (First Line)

1. **Question**: "Are you a [audience] struggling with [problem]?"
2. **Bold Claim**: "[Specific result] in [timeframe]. Here's how."
3. **Stat**: "[X]% of [audience] are losing [amount] because of [mistake]."
4. **Story**: "Last month, one of our clients [specific outcome]..."
5. **Callout**: "Attention [specific audience]:"

## Copy Frameworks

### PAS (Problem-Agitation-Solution)
- **Problem**: State the pain
- **Agitation**: Make it worse, show cost of inaction
- **Solution**: Present the offer as the fix

### AIDA (Attention-Interest-Desire-Action)
- **Attention**: Hook/interrupt
- **Interest**: Expand on the problem or opportunity
- **Desire**: Show proof and outcomes
- **Action**: Clear CTA

## Rules
- First line must stop the scroll
- Qualify the audience early (repel wrong people)
- One CTA per ad
- Specific numbers > vague claims
- Test 3–5 variants per campaign (different hooks)
- Match ad voice to landing page voice
