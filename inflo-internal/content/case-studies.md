## Headline Copy Patterns (Voice Reference)

These headlines follow a consistent formula:
1. **Bold result number** as the hook — "$36k", "$30,266", "$6,000+", "$5k days"
2. **Time constraint** to amplify the result — "in 30 days", "in 18 days", "on a Tuesday", "on a Saturday"
3. **Subheadline attributes it to the system**, not luck — "repeatable systems at scale", "proper systems and a team", "one setter, one closer, and a system"

What the headlines NEVER do:
- No hype words ("amazing", "incredible", "crushing it")
- No emojis in the headline
- No vague claims — always a specific dollar amount
- No "we're so great" — it's always about the system producing the result
