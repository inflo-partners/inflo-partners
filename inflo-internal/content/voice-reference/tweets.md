## Voice Notes

- One sentence per line, double-spaced
- Short, punchy, conversational
- Leads with a bold or contrarian statement
- Uses real numbers and math to prove the point
- CTAs are simple: "comment X, I'll send it"
- No hype, no fluff, no motivational talk
- Speaks as matter-of-fact, not opinion
- Engagement hooks: repost + comment + must be following
- Never use em dashes (—) or en dashes (–), use commas or separate lines instead
