Open Tally → Create new form → Title: "Club Kaya — Onboarding Assessment"

Welcome to Club Kaya. This assessment helps us build your personalized performance system. Please answer every question honestly and thoroughly — the more detail you give, the better your program will be. This takes about 10-15 minutes.

- I am aware of reducing blue light exposure in the evening, but I am not very strict with it (sometimes blue light blockers, I lower the lights in the evening, I try not to scroll in bed)

## After Building

1. Click Publish
2. Grab the form link
3. In Tally settings, connect the form to Zapier (Tally has native Zapier integration)
4. In the Everfit Auto Flow, add an auto message on Day 1 with the Tally link
5. Zap 1 trigger becomes: Tally → New Submission
