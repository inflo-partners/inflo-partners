## Mechanism
- **USP / Unique Mechanism:** Guy and my personalities are very data driven. We look at data going into the deal, but we also continue to look at data as we have the project owned. We manage 3rd party managers closely to make sure we're not a month behind if something isn't going right.

We currently underwrite 40% of the deals that come onto the market today in the country. We are looking to grow that to 100% by the end of the year by leveraging Ai and modifying our review process.

We strive to be connected to every owner, wholesaler and broker in the country. This will keep our finger on the pulse of this asset class in the country which will allow us to better decide which facilities to buy, for how much and when to buy. It will also put us on the leading edge of knowledge for when we can apply for refinancing or selling our facilities as we see changes happening.
- **Why Traditional Approaches Fail:** Many people don't look at the entire picture when purchasing a facility. There are many factors to consider. We like to stay on top of things to be able to pivot where needed.
- **Problem Solved:** Our investors are looking grow their wealth beyond traditional methods of investing in the stock market. Some are also looking for tax benefits, like depreciation, to offset their income. What we provide is a tangible, real estate product that has shown to be resistant to recession.
