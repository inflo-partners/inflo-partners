## Email 2: Why Self-Storage (And Why Us)

**Send Timing:** Day before the call
**Goal:** Reinforce asset class thesis, present the data-driven USP, address the trust objection with proof

---

**Subject:** Why self-storage outperformed in COVID
**Preview Text:** And what our underwriting process looks like.

---

Hi {{first_name}},

Your briefing is tomorrow. Before we connect, one thing worth understanding:

**Why self-storage holds up when other asset classes don't.**

During COVID, our self-storage portfolio grew. Our rental properties did not.

That's not luck. It's the nature of the asset class.

Self-storage demand is driven by three groups that don't go away in a downturn:

- **Business users** — inventory, tools, equipment storage
- **Transient users** — people moving, students, life transitions
- **Emotional attachment users** — parents downsizing, estate situations

None of these three groups disappear in a recession. That's the structural advantage.

**What separates Momentum Wealth Fund from other operators:**

- We currently underwrite **40% of all self-storage facilities coming to market nationally**, and we're building toward **100% coverage by year-end using AI**
- We maintain direct relationships with owners, wholesalers, and brokers across the country — this gives us deal flow and market intelligence most funds don't have access to
- We manage third-party operators closely and review performance data on an ongoing basis — we're not hands-off once a deal closes
- We deliberately avoid high-risk geographies (Gulf Coast, parts of Florida) due to insurance exposure — risk mitigation is part of the underwriting, not an afterthought

**Our track record to date:**
