Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Mucci Strength LLC ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and handles your personal information when you visit our website, fill out a form, or interact with our services. By using our website, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

We collect personal information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Any additional details you submit through our application or inquiry forms

This information is collected through our website forms and third-party tools we use to operate our services, including scheduling and application platforms.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your inquiries and communicate with you
  • To schedule and confirm coaching consultations
  • To provide and deliver our coaching services
  • To send you relevant communications, updates, and follow-ups related to our services
  • To improve and optimize our website and service offerings

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate our business. Each service processes your data in accordance with their own privacy policies:

  • Typeform — Used for application and intake forms. View Typeform's privacy policy at typeform.com/privacy.
  • Calendly — Used for scheduling consultations. View Calendly's privacy policy at calendly.com/privacy.
  • Meta Pixel — Used for advertising and conversion tracking on Facebook and Instagram. View Meta's data policy at facebook.com/privacy/policy.
  • Microsoft Clarity — Used for website analytics and session recordings to help us understand how visitors use our site. View Microsoft's privacy policy at privacy.microsoft.com.

We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these third-party services to understand how they handle your data.

Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies for analytics and advertising purposes. These technologies help us understand how visitors interact with our site, measure the performance of our advertising campaigns, and improve the overall user experience.

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of certain features on our website. For instructions on managing cookies in your browser, consult your browser's help documentation.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, or as required by applicable law. When your information is no longer needed, we will delete or anonymize it in a secure manner.

You can request deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us at the address below. We will process your request in accordance with applicable privacy laws.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@muccistrength.com. We will respond to your request within a reasonable timeframe.

Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for adults and are not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected information from someone under 18, we will take steps to delete that information promptly.

If you believe we may have collected information from a minor, please contact us at info@muccistrength.com.
